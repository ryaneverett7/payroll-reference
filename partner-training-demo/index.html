An outgoing deduction transmission failed for James Carter. The Medical deduction of $45.00 was not received by payroll. This typically occurs when the employee record in payroll doesn't match the identifier in Employee Navigator.
How to Resolve:
1
Go to Deduction Audit → Discrepancies and locate the failed record for James Carter.
2
Verify the employee's payroll ID or identifier matches exactly between Employee Navigator and the payroll system.
3
Once corrected, click Send next to the record to re-transmit the deduction to payroll.
4
The record will move to In-Sync and this Needs Action alert will clear automatically.

We're On It — Partner API Timeout
Auto-retry scheduled for tonight at 11:00 PM ET
Auto-Retry
The partner's API timed out during the last scheduled transmission. This can happen during peak processing times or if the partner system is temporarily unavailable. No action is required from you — Employee Navigator will automatically retry the transmission tonight.
What Happens Next:
1
Employee Navigator will automatically retry the failed transmission tonight at 11:00 PM ET.
2
If successful, the We're On It card will clear and affected records will move to In-Sync.
3
If the retry also fails, the issue will move to Needs Action and you'll receive a notification to take manual steps.
